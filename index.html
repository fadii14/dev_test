WHO WE ARE?

Tarrabin Consulting Ltd. is a leading management consulting firm located in the Wood Buffalo region of Alberta. Our company is dedicated to providing innovative and practical solutions to corporations, mid-size, and small businesses, and indigenous communities in various industries such as oil and gas, healthcare, and more. With our team of experienced consultants, we aim to provide exceptional services that meet our clients’ needs and exceed their expectations.

WHAT WE DO?

Tarrabin Consulting LTD is a leading business consulting firm in the Wood Buffalo region. Our services include entrepreneur support, business planning, and HR recruitment. With over 25 years of experience, our team is equipped with the knowledge and skills to help organizations reach their goals.

We understand that every business is unique, and that’s why we take a personalized approach to each project. Our team works closely with clients to understand their needs and develop customized solutions that deliver results.

Our business planning services provide practical and effective strategies for growth and success, while our HR recruitment services help organizations find the best talent for their needs.
